# Tier 2 Batch Enhancement Strategy

## Proven 5-Layer Pattern (Validated on 6 files)

Each file enhancement follows this structure:

### Layer 1: Beginner Mental Model (10-15 min)
- Real-world analogy (familiar concept)
- Why it matters (business impact + examples)
- Visual simple explanation
- Add at top after title

### Layer 2: Intermediate (already in most files)
- How it works, lifecycle, decision trees
- Usually exists in original content
- Enhance with better structure

### Layer 3: Advanced (already in most files)
- Deep internals, optimization, scaling
- Usually exists in original content
- Link to other concepts

### Layer 4: Production Reality (10-15 min)
- 6-8 failure modes table
- Real production incident (2-3 paragraphs)
- Observability/debugging

### Layer 5: Staff Perspective (5-10 min)
- Tradeoff table
- Scaling patterns
- Architecture evolution story

### Bonus: Interview Questions (10 min)
- Level 1 (junior): 2 questions
- Level 2 (intermediate): 2 questions
- Level 3 (senior): 2 questions
- Level 4 (staff): 2 questions

**Total per file: 45-60 minutes**
