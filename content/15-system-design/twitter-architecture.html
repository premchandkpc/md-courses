Twitter/X Architecture
500M MAU · 500M tweets/day · Hybrid Timeline
Twitter serves 500M+ users with a hybrid push/pull timeline — normal users get precomputed feeds via fan-out on write, while celebrities use on-demand reads to prevent fan-out meltdown. Cassandra stores tweets and graph; Redis caches timelines, trends, and hot data; Kafka decouples fan-out delivery; Earlybird powers real-time search.
