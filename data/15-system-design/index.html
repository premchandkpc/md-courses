🎓 Interview Structure

Duration: 3-4 hours for full depth. 45 minutes per component.

  1. Requirements Clarification: Restate functional & non-functional requirements. Push on assumptions.
  2. Estimation: QPS, storage, compute. Show your math.
  3. Architecture: System diagram. Request flow. Service boundaries.
  4. Components: Deep dive on 3-4 critical components (retrieval, ranking, features).
  5. ML Specifics: Model architecture, training, online learning, exploration/exploitation.
  6. Production Reality: Failures, monitoring, A/B testing, rollout strategy.
  7. Scaling Challenges: What breaks at 10x, 100x? How do you optimize?
