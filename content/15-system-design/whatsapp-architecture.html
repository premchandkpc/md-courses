WhatsApp
2B+ users · 100B msg/day · E2EE · Multi-device
WhatsApp is a post office that never reads your letters — persistent connections carry sealed armored envelopes (E2EE), the server only knows who is home and where to deliver. Designed for 1.2M msg/sec peak, 99.99% availability, and battery-efficient mobile-first operation.

Message Delivery Flow

End-to-End Path

From sender tap to recipient screen: message is encrypted via Double Ratchet, sent over persistent WebSocket/TCP, routed through Connection Manager to recipient's CM (or queued + push notified if offline), stored in Cassandra wide-row, and acknowledged with delivery receipts at each stage.
