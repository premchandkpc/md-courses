Uber Architecture
Real-time Dispatch · Geospatial Indexing · Pricing Engine · 10M+ Rides/Day
Uber is a real-time taxi dispatcher that knows where every driver is, predicts where riders will appear, and adjusts prices like an airline booking system. The city is divided into hexagons (H3). When you request a ride, the dispatcher finds the nearest free driver in your hex and neighboring cells — the first to accept wins.
