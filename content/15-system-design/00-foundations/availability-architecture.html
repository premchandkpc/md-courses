🔄 Active-Active

Both regions serve traffic simultaneously. Requires global load balancer, conflict-free DB replication, and stateless apps.

📋 Health Checks

ELB health checks every 5-15s, application-level health endpoints (/health, /ready), circuit breakers detect cascading failures.

⚡ Graceful Degradation

Feature flags to disable non-critical features, cached stale data when DB is down, read-only mode.

📊 SLA Math

99.9% = 8.7h downtime/year. 99.99% = 52min/year. 99.999% = 5min/year. Each 9 adds ~10× cost complexity.

RedundancyActive-Active (both live) vs Active-Passive (standby takes over)
